Wood Grain Effect

As aesthetic values evolve, wood grain effect finishing is now becoming more and more popular in the fields of architectural decoration, furniture, electronics and home appliances. Wood grain effect finishing is a special powder coating process based on the latest technology in the surface finishing.

The wood grain finished aluminium profile is a perfect integration of the excellent aluminium properties with the natural appearance of natural resources such as wood, marble, etc. It is a wonderful combination of the state-of-the-art technology and aesthetics. The products have stable quality, appealing appearance and therefore a higher value of decoration.

Wood Grain Effect Features

Materials:

The wood pattern coating can be cured and transferred to the surface of aluminum profile or aluminum sheet under a temperature of 200 degree Celsius

Shapes:

The wood pattern can be transferred to all kinds of substrate shapes, including sheets.

Effects:

The decorative effects of pictures, text and graphs, and all kinds of natural sceneries (such as wood, marble or granite grains) can be achieved through photographic technology.

Wood Finish Powder Coating Services

Wood finish powder coating, why it makes sense to opt for the same

When it comes to retrofitting your office, you can opt for powder coating wood finish, and give it the natural appearance of wood, with grain included. From a monetary point of view, the powder coating wood finish should cost a lot less using aluminum as a base and utilizing powder coating wood finish to get it evenly coated, while giving it the appearance of fresh varnished wood. The process for wood grain powder coating starts with coating aluminum with the powder by utilizing an electrostatic spray gun, which enables the powder to evenly coat the base material. Once the wood grain powder coating is set, it is then heated to ensure that the wood grain finishing powder coating is bonded to the material and becomes permanently set. With powder coating wood finish, you can ensure that your home stands out for the right reason.

With wood grain powder coating, you can now opt to dress up various shapes in aluminum and coat it to look like natural Oak, Redwood and even Marble. It should enable you to redo your office or residence, for much less and with powder coating wood finish, you should be able to get any aluminum base to resemble any wood, including teak. You can opt for various tones to get the right preferred one for your aluminum based windows and doors and get it retrofitted using powder coating wood finish.

While you can opt to use original materials for decorating your office or home, it can be quite cost prohibitive, especially when you opt for marble. This is why it makes better sense to opt for wood grain finishing powder coating as it is not cost prohibitive and offers you a wide range of shades and tones so that you can make an informed decision. Moreover, with wood grain finishing powder coating, you should be able to get that floor to look like the real Marble.
